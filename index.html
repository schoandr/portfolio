About Andrew

Upon leaving high school I was committed to becoming a graphic designer, however, upon learning the difficulties involved with finding work due to the job saturation in the field, I pivoted into seasonal employment working between Alaska and Colorado as a commercial driver. Doing this gave me the time to reflect on myself and decide what I wanted to do with my life. As I neared thirty, a close friend began to talk to me about web development, and so my journey with Thinkful began.

For the longest time, I had wanted to get into an IT-related field. I'm excited to be able to work with Thinkful in pursuing this goal. Gaining some independence from the typical 9 - 5 style jobs is undeniably nice. But I'm enjoying my adventure into web development, to be able to work and collaborate with a team, and I'm excited for what's to come!
